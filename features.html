Our app works by utilizing tired and trusted methods for P2P encrypted communications that effectively make all your communications invisible. In today's modern world we have seen that the press and journalists have been actively targets by police and military forces around the world to try and suppress the freedom of information. This is abhorrent and the main reasons why our services have been developed


When we say Exclusivity over your communications we mean complete Exclusivity. There have been many instances where people have been thinking they have been communicating with a trusted source, when in reality the trusted source has been compromised resulting in the user being doxed and having their information revealed. This is another method that the state will use to try and suppress the freedom of information


The way in which this app and our services mitigate the aforementioned security concerns is by vetting and controlling the entire network that the app runs on. P2P networking only works when tallest 50% of the network remains uncompressed, therefore we make sure that our worldwide servers are all hidden and heavily encrypted and that anyone that wishes to use our services must go through extensive vetting to maintain the security of our network. No information will be gathered on the user and we ourselves do not know any of the encryption keys meaning that we cannot, even under threat from the government, give out the encryption keys.


Our app has built-in fail-safes meaning that in the event of tampering or hacking, it will self destruct. We employ some of the worlds best cryptographers and blackhat consultants to make sure our app and its features remain up to date and have no 0-day exploits.
